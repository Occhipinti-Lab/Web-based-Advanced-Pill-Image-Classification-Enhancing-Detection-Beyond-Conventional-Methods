Web-based Advanced Pill Image Classification: Enhancing Detection Beyond Conventional Methods

-------------------------------

About The Project

Welcome to our pill image classification project. Leveraging the power of transfer learning with ResNet and DenseNet architectures, our model accurately identifies various pill types from images. With our user-friendly web app, you can easily upload an image and receive instant predictions, aiding in medication identification and safety.

Key Features

Accurate Classification

Our model utilizes state-of-the-art transfer learning techniques to achieve high accuracy in identifying pill types, ensuring reliable results.

User-Friendly Interface

Our web app provides a seamless experience, allowing users to effortlessly upload images and receive quick predictions, making medication identification convenient and accessible.

Safety and Convenience

By leveraging advanced image classification technology, our project promotes medication safety by assisting users in verifying their pills, empowering them to make informed decisions about their health.
